Team Sanki Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 22, 2024

Introduction:
Welcome to Team Sanki! This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you interact with our bot, @TS_XD_MUSIC_BOT, and use our services. By using Team Sanki, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Data Collection and Storage

Data Collected:

  • Chat IDs and User IDs: We collect and store chat IDs and user IDs where the bot is used. This data is essential for the bot's functionality.
  • Current Activity Data: We track activities like commands issued and services requested.
  • Bot Statistics: We store usage data such as the number of active users, groups, and commands executed.

Data Storage:
All collected data is stored securely in our backend systems and databases. It is updated regularly to ensure accurate operations and may be stored permanently for future improvements.

2. Data Usage

Functionality: The primary purpose of data collection is to facilitate the smooth functioning of Team Sanki, including processing commands and managing bot operations within Telegram groups.

Analyzing Usage Patterns: We analyze data to improve performance and plan updates.

Sharing with Advertisers: General usage statistics are shared publicly via the /stats command. These statistics are aggregated and do not include personal identifiers.

3. Data Protection

Security Measures: We implement industry-standard security practices to protect your data. It is stored securely in backend systems and databases to prevent unauthorized access.

Restricted Access: Only the owner and authorized personnel of Team Sanki, @TS_XD_MUSIC_BOT, and the admin @TSGCODER have access to the data.

4. User Rights

Data Removal Requests: You can request deletion of your personal data or group data by contacting us via the support group, @TSGCODER.

Group Data Removal: If requesting removal of group data, proof of admin status is required.

Privacy Concerns: For any concerns, contact us via @TSGCODER.

5. Minors' Data

Intended Use: Team Sanki is accessible to all Telegram users, including minors. However, we do not have specific features to prevent minors from accessing inappropriate content.

Data Handling: We handle all data equally, regardless of the user's age.

6. Changes to the Privacy Policy

Policy Updates: This privacy policy may be updated to reflect changes in our practices.

Notification: Updates will be announced via our official channel, Team Sanki Network. We encourage users to review the policy periodically.

7. Data Controller

Ownership and Control: Data collected by Team Sanki is controlled and accessed exclusively by @TS_XD_MUSIC_BOT and @TSGCODER. For questions or concerns, contact us via @TSGCODER.

Conclusion

Your privacy is very important to us, and we are committed to handling your data responsibly and securely. By continuing to use Team Sanki, you agree to the terms outlined in this privacy policy.
